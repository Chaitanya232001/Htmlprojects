Cricket Rules

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a circular field. The game is played by running between two sets of wickets at the center of the field.

Test Format

The Test format is the longest format of the game, played over five days. Each team gets two innings to bat. Test cricket emphasizes skill and endurance.

One Day Format

One Day cricket is played in a single day, with each team getting a limited number of overs (usually 50). It is known for its pace and strategy.

T20 Format

T20 cricket is the shortest format, with each team playing 20 overs. It is known for its fast-paced, exciting style and is popular in various cricket leagues.
