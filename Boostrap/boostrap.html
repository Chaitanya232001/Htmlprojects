The surroundings we live in, the natural resources or food we consume all are parts of nature. Nature provides a sustainable environment and necessary resources for survival like air, water, soil etc. Nature helps to flourish the ecosystem and biodiversity of our planet by providing all the necessary resources. Trees, plants, and forests are the vital parts of nature which provide Oxygen.

The chirping of birds, the buzzing of insects, and rustling of leaves are the sounds of nature which relax our mind and calm our soul. Nature is the main source of food, be it dairy, grains, fruits, or nuts, all come from Mother Nature. The clothes we wear to cover our body and get saved from extreme weather conditions also come from nature.

Water is one of the vital requirements for all the known forms of life, and nature has provided it in an enormous amount to us. The selfishness and greed of man have made nature vulnerable to increasing pollution. The fierce response of nature in the past few years has made us realize that if we do not stop the destruction of nature, it will raise the question of human survival.
